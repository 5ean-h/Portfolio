About Us

The goal of this club is to help people at all levels and ages to progress further in skating. Polishing their current skills and having fun hanging out with people with common interest. Skating is a recreation sport simular to cycling or rollerskating however with the goal being slightly different. In skating without any handle bars you have to focus and control your board with your feet.
