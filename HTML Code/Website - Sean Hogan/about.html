Info About Us

What is skating?

Skating is a recreation sport simular to cycling or rollerskating however with the goal being slightly different. In skating without any handle bars you have to focus and control your board with your feet. As you progress in skating you will learn how to do trick which are different ways to manipulate the directions of your board. Ramps are also a big part of skating. Learning to keep your balance and do tricks while moving in strange directions.

What is the goal of the club?

The goal of this club is to help people at all levels and ages to progress further in skating. Polishing their current skills and having fun hanging out with people with common interest.

What do I need to join?

All you need to join is a skateboard. You can join at anytime and all ages are welcome. We recommond knee pads and helmets for younger people at the skatepark for safety. You can do a trail run at the beggining to see if you have interest. Hope to see you soon!!
