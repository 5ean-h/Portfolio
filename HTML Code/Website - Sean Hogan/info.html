Some Skating Info

Ollie

This is a trick to raise your board from the ground. To do an ollie you hit your tail off the ground and slide your foot upwards.

Kickflip

A kickflip is like an ollie but you kick the board slightly to the side creating a spin.

Shuvit

The shuvit is turning your board 180 on the x axis while riding the board. You do this by kicking down and away on the tail.

Heelflip

A heelflip is very simular to a kickflip but instead of kicking with the top of your foot you use your heel.

Treflip / 360 Flip

A treflip is a trick that moves in a 360 on the y axis. You do this trick by moving your front foot up like an ollie and backfoot back like a shuvit.

Powerslide

A powerslide is when you move paralell to the direction you are moving and slide. To do this you lift your weigth slightly, turn your board and lean back.
